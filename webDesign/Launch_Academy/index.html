About Me

Hello Launch Academy! My name is Chase Blokker, and I'm 23 years old. Last year I received a major in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science at Loyola Marymount University. Through my EE coursework, I was slowly introduced to CS. I enjoyed it enough to make it my minor. But it wasn't until I took classes in Interaction Design and Computer Graphics when I really fell in love with CS. I realized that I loved javascript and the web space. Therefore, after receiving my EE degree, I decided to pursue a major in CS, which I'm currently doing right now.

I know exactly what I want to do with CS, which is to become a full stack web developer. Unfortunately, I feel that my CS curriculum doesn't fully expose me to the amazing modern web frameworks - like ruby on rails and backbone.js. This is why I'm applying to Launch Academy, so I can quickly learn skills and best practices to become a kick ass web developer!

I also have an eye for design, which hopefully comes across in this website. I enjoy discussing what makes a graphical user interface look beautiful and user-friendly.

I have some experience with HTML, CSS, and Javascript, as well as some backend work in MongoDB, using the bottle.py web framework.

I have limited experience working in groups, and I believe this is my biggest hurdle. Launch Academy will give me the opportunity to develop good pair programming practices, as well as help me learn about the agile development process.

I think all the above factors make me a perfect candidate for Launch Academy. I have some coding experience with an eye for design, but I have a lot of room to grow with respect to working with groups and truly becoming confident in my full stack skills need to bundle all my knowledge together so I can learn how to build awesome modern web apps!
